6D Pose Estimation and 3D Reconstruction of Industrial Physical Twin and Digital Twin Datasets

CESI — CESI LINEACT  |  FUSION Project

Abstract

We present a novel dataset and methodology for 6D pose estimation and 3D reconstruction of industrial objects, designed to bridge the gap between physical twin and digital twin representations. Our dataset captures real-world industrial components under diverse conditions, paired with high-fidelity 3D digital twin models, enabling rigorous benchmarking of pose estimation and reconstruction algorithms in industrial settings. This work supports the FUSION project at CESI LINEACT, advancing the integration of physical and digital worlds for smart manufacturing applications.
